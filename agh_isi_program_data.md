## PROGRAM DESCRIPTION

The ISI program focuses on theoretical and practical use of artificial intelligence methods. The curriculum also includes general computer science, mathematics, and physics courses. Graduates are prepared for careers as programmers, software engineers, database specialists, data analysts, business analysts, software architects, and many other roles in the IT industry.

The program responds to increasing market demand for specialists who can combine fundamental computer science education with skills in data analysis, machine learning, analysis and design of complex information systems, and successfully apply these in software development processes.

**Internship:** After the 6th semester, students complete a minimum 4-week internship (120 hours) at an IT company. International internships available through CERN and Erasmus+ programs.
